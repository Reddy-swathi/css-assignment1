SAVORING HYDERABAD

Biryanis

Hyderabad is famous for its biryani, a fragrant and flavorful rice dish. Hyderabadi biryani is known for its unique cooking method called "dum" where marinated meat and partially cooked rice are layered and cooked together in a sealed pot, allowing the flavors to blend perfectly.



The key ingredients include basmati rice, tender meat (usually chicken or mutton), yogurt, onions, spices like saffron, cardamom, and cloves, and fresh herbs like mint and coriander. The result is a rich, aromatic, and delicious dish that's loved by many around the world.

Osmania Biscuit

Osmania biscuits are a popular snack from Hyderabad, loved for their unique taste and texture. These biscuits are slightly sweet and mildly salty, with a crumbly and buttery consistency. Named after the last Nizam of Hyderabad, Mir Osman Ali Khan, they are often enjoyed with tea.




Made from simple ingredients like flour, sugar, butter, and milk, Osmania biscuits are a delightful treat that captures the essence of Hyderabadi hospitality and tradition.


Iranian Chai

Iranian chai, or Irani chai, is a famous tea in Hyderabad known for its rich and creamy flavor. This tea is prepared by brewing tea leaves separately and then mixing them with thick, boiled milk and sugar. Irani chai is typically served in small glasses and pairs wonderfully with local snacks like Osmania biscuits.
