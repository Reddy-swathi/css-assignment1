Pachi Pulusu

Pachi Pulusu is a traditional dish from the South Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It's a type of raw tamarind soup made with tamarind pulp, onions, green chilies, jaggery, and spices.



Unlike other soups or stews, Pachi Pulusu is not cooked, which gives it a unique, fresh flavor. It's usually served with rice and can be refreshing, especially during hot weather.

Ragi Sangati

Ragi Sangati is a traditional dish from South India, particularly popular in the states of Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka. It is made from ragi (finger millet) flour and rice. This wholesome and nutritious dish is often served with spicy curries, like chicken curry or vegetable sambar.



Ragi Sangati is known for its health benefits, including being high in calcium, iron, and fiber, making it a great option for a balanced diet.


Bagara Baigan

Baghara Baingan is a popular dish from the Indian state of Hyderabad. It features small eggplants (baingan) cooked in a rich, spicy, and tangy gravy made from peanuts, sesame seeds, coconut, and various spices.
